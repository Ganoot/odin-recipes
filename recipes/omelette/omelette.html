What is omelette?

A dish made from beaten eggs cooked in a pan, often with various fillings like cheese, vegetables, or meat, and then folded over.

Ingredients

  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • ½ teaspoon cold water
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch cayenne or white pepper to taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Pour the eggs into a hot pan.
  3. Gently fold the edges into fluff up the omelette base.
  4. Cover with lid and let it cook on medium for 2 minutes.
  5. Flip the omelette and let cook for another minutes.
  6. Serve onto a plate and enjoy, bread / toast optional.
